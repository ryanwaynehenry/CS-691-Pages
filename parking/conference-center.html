Conference Center Parking Guide

Lot access, rates, and entry directions.

BENIGN

Entry Points

  • North Gate: badge or QR code scan
  • West Garage: visitor kiosk check-in
  • South Overflow Lot: event-only parking

Accessibility

  • ADA spaces available on Level 1 and Level 2
  • Elevator access near bays A12 and C03
  • Drop-off lane at Main Lobby entrance

Duration Rate Notes
