How to Pay

Pay stations are located at each garage exit lane and beside the stairwells on every level.

  1. Locate a blue pay kiosk before returning to your vehicle.
  2. Scan the barcode on your entry ticket, or type in your license plate number.
  3. Review the calculated fee shown on the display screen.
  4. Pay by credit / debit card or contactless tap. Cash is not accepted.
  5. Collect your exit receipt. You have 15 minutes to reach the exit gate after payment.
Conference Validation: Get your parking ticket stamped at the Registration Desk in Main Lobby (Level 1) to receive the discounted attendee rate. Validation is available during all scheduled sessions.

Entry Points

  • North Gate — badge or QR code scan
  • West Garage — visitor kiosk check-in
  • South Overflow Lot C — event-only access
  • Main Lobby Drop-Off Lane — 10-minute maximum

Accessibility

  • ADA spaces on Level 1 (bays A12 – A18) and Level 2
  • Elevator access near bays A12 and C03
  • Accessible drop-off at Main Lobby entrance
  • Mobility assistance: call ext. 4400 from any lobby phone
