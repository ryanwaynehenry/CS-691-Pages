How to Connect

  1. Open your device's Wi-Fi settings (Settings → Wi-Fi, or swipe down to Control Center).
  2. Select Venue-Guest from the list of nearby networks.
  3. Enter the password Welcome2026 and tap Join.
  4. Open any browser. A Guest Policy page will appear — scroll to the bottom and tap Accept & Connect to activate internet access.
  5. You're connected. The session stays active for the duration of the event; no re-authentication is required unless you leave the venue.

If the policy page doesn't appear automatically, navigate to any http:// (non-HTTPS) address to trigger the captive portal.

Coverage Areas

Main Hall (Level 1)

Full coverage — stage, seating, and exhibit floor.

Lobby & Atrium

Strong signal at entry, registration desk, and near elevators.

Breakout Rooms (Level 2)

Individual access points in rooms B201 – B214. May require a one-time re-authentication.

Courtyard

Partial outdoor coverage. Best within 30 ft of the building exterior.

Guest Network Policy

  • Provided for general browsing and event-related use.
  • Avoid large media downloads — bandwidth is shared among all attendees.
  • Do not attempt to access other devices connected to the same network.
  • Traffic is monitored for capacity management only; no personal data is logged.

Troubleshooting

ProblemSolution
Policy page never appears Disconnect, reconnect, then open an http:// URL (not HTTPS) to trigger the portal.
Password rejected Confirm you selected Venue-Guest (not a similarly named network) and re-type the password exactly.
Weak signal in a breakout room Move near the access point mounted above the door frame, or ask staff to check the room repeater.
Connected but no internet The policy acceptance step may have timed out. Forget the network and start the connection from scratch.

For hands-on help, visit the AV & Tech Desk in Lobby C, open 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM.
