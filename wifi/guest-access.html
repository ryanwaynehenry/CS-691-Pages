Guest Wi-Fi Access

Quick setup instructions for event visitors.

BENIGN

Network: `Venue-Guest` | Password: `Welcome2026`

Connection Steps

  1. Open your device Wi-Fi settings.
  2. Select Venue-Guest.
  3. Enter password Welcome2026.
  4. Open a browser and accept the guest policy page.

Troubleshooting

  • If the policy page does not appear, reconnect and retry.
  • Move closer to hall access points if signal is weak.
  • Visit the help desk in Lobby C for device assistance.
